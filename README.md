# 隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

**最后更新日期：2024年3月10日**

本隐私政策说明了 **CyberDocker**（以下简称“本应用”）如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。我们非常重视您的隐私，并致力于保护您的个人数据安全。

## 1. 我们收集的信息 (Information We Collect)

### 1.1 个人数据 (Personal Data)
本应用**不会**收集、存储或传输任何个人身份信息（如姓名、地址、电话号码、电子邮件地址等）。您在使用本应用时产生的所有数据均仅存储在您的本地设备上。

### 1.2 系统数据 (System Data)
为了提供核心功能（如系统资源监控），本应用需要访问以下本地系统信息：
- **CPU 使用率**：仅用于实时显示，不记录或上传。
- **内存使用情况**：仅用于实时显示，不记录或上传。
- **电池状态**：仅用于实时显示，不记录或上传。
- **磁盘读写活动**：仅用于实时显示，不记录或上传。

### 1.3 用户文件与快捷方式 (User Files & Shortcuts)
本应用允许您添加应用程序、文件夹或文件的快捷方式。这些路径信息仅保存在您的本地设备（UserDefaults）中，用于为您提供快速启动功能。我们不会访问、读取或上传您文件中的具体内容。

## 2. 数据存储与安全 (Data Storage & Security)

所有用户配置（如固定的应用列表、界面设置等）均存储在您设备的本地沙盒环境（Sandbox）中。我们没有任何远程服务器，也不会将您的任何数据上传至云端或第三方服务器。

## 3. 权限使用说明 (Permissions)

本应用可能会请求以下权限以实现特定功能：
- **文件访问权限**：当您选择添加文件或文件夹快捷方式时，我们需要获取该特定路径的只读访问权限（通过 Security-Scoped Bookmarks），以便在应用重启后仍能为您打开该文件。
- **自动化控制 (Apple Events)**：用于控制 Finder 以打开特定文件夹或应用程序。

## 4. 第三方服务 (Third-Party Services)
本应用**不包含**任何第三方分析工具、广告 SDK 或追踪代码。我们不会与任何第三方共享您的数据。

## 5. 儿童隐私 (Children's Privacy)
本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童。由于我们不收集任何个人信息，因此也不涉及儿童个人信息的收集问题。

# Privacy Policy (English Version)

**Last Updated: March 10, 2024**

This Privacy Policy explains how **CyberDocker** ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
The App **does not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as names, addresses, phone numbers, email addresses, etc.). All data generated while using the App is stored solely on your local device.

### 1.2 System Data
To provide core features (such as system resource monitoring), the App requires access to the following local system information:
- **CPU Usage**: Used only for real-time display; not recorded or uploaded.
- **Memory Usage**: Used only for real-time display; not recorded or uploaded.
- **Battery Status**: Used only for real-time display; not recorded or uploaded.
- **Disk Activity**: Used only for real-time display; not recorded or uploaded.

### 1.3 User Files & Shortcuts
The App allows you to add shortcuts to applications, folders, or files. These path details are saved locally on your device (in UserDefaults) to provide quick launch functionality. We do not access, read, or upload the content of your files.

## 2. Data Storage & Security

All user configurations (such as pinned app lists, interface settings, etc.) are stored within the local Sandbox environment on your device. We do not operate any remote servers and do not upload any of your data to the cloud or third-party servers.

## 3. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to enable specific features:
- **File Access**: When you choose to add file or folder shortcuts, we require read-only access to that specific path (via Security-Scoped Bookmarks) to open the file for you after an app restart.
- **Automation (Apple Events)**: Used to control Finder for opening specific folders or applications.

## 4. Third-Party Services
The App **does not contain** any third-party analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking code. We do not share your data with any third parties.

## 5. Children's Privacy
The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we also do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
